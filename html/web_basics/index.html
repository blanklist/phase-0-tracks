The Adventures of Shoo-Shoo and Y-Y!

Shoo-Shoo and Y-Y! were a couple of cats that got into trouble pretty much on the daily. It's not that either of them intended to be such trouble makers. You could say it was in their nature.

Some examples of troublesome behavior:

  • Knocking things off of counters (especially pens)
  • Opening cupboards
  • Rooting through cupboards
  • Harassment of humans while sitting at computer

Disciplinary actions and level of effectiveness

  1. Say name in loud voice. Ineffective
  2. Squirt with water bottle. Momentarily effective
  3. Put in spare room. Effective with side effect of loud meowing
Beware!
